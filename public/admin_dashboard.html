3-Pool Profit Distribution Engine

How distributable profit gets split across partners on each project.

60%
Funding Index
Capital contribution proportion
20%
Effort Index
Avg. peer ratings (1–10) post-project
20%
Deal Bonus Index
Goes to the partner who sourced it

Reserve fund holds 40% of gross profit before distribution. The remaining 60% gets split via the three indices above. Final partner share = (Fund% × 60% + Effort% × 20% + Deal% × 20%) × Distributable Profit, less 10% tax withholding.
