Live profit calculations and distribution tracking will appear here once the expenses, invoices, and project_financials tables are added to Supabase. The 3-pool engine below is the design preview.

3-Pool Profit Distribution Engine

How distributable profit gets split across partners on each project.

60%
Funding Index
Capital contribution proportion
20%
Effort Index
Avg. peer ratings (1–10) post-project
20%
Deal Bonus Index

Reserve fund holds 40% of gross profit before distribution. The remaining 60% gets split via the three indices above. Final partner share = (Fund% + Effort% + Deal%) × Distributable Profit, less 10% tax withholding.
