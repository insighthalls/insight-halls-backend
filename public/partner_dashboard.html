How Your Share Is Calculated

Three pools combine to determine each partner's profit share.

60%
Funding Index
Your capital contribution share
20%
Effort Index
Avg. peer ratings (1–10)
20%
Deal Bonus
For project sourcer

Reserve fund holds 40% of gross profit. The remaining 60% distributable pool is split across partners using the three indices. Final share = (Fund% × 60% + Effort% × 20% + Deal% × 20%) × Distributable Profit, less 10% tax withholding.
